Welcome to the Better Web Conference 2024

The premier event for front-end developers! Join us in Gothenburg from October 18th to 20th for three action-packed days of cutting-edge seminars, hands-on workshops, and expert keynotes. Learn about the latest trends in CSS, JavaScript, Web3, and performance optimization, while connecting with industry leaders. Whether you're improving your skills in design systems or mastering Progressive Web Apps, this conference is your gateway to building better, faster, and more accessible websites. Don't miss out on the future of front-end development!
